About us

For the past 10 years, AnimalsGo Shelter has been a beacon of hope for homeless, abandoned, and injured animals. Founded on the principles of love, care, and responsibility, AnimalsGo has dedicated itself to rescuing animals in need, rehabilitating them, and finding them safe, loving homes. Operating with a team of passionate volunteers and professionals, AnimalsGo provides essential services such as medical care, vaccination, and socialization programs for the animals. The shelter also runs awareness campaigns to educate the community about responsible pet ownership and the importance of animal welfare. Over the last decade, AnimalsGo has successfully rehomed thousands of animals, ensuring they receive the second chance they deserve. Its commitment to sustainability and growth has made it a cornerstone of the community, inspiring others to join the mission of creating a kinder world for animals. AnimalsGo looks forward to many more years of making a difference in the lives of animals and the people who care for them. Learn more about AnimalsGo and its work by visiting their website or reaching out to their dedicated team of animal advocates.

Animals we have

At AnimalsGo, every animal finds a temporary home and a chance for a better life. We provide care, medical treatment, food, and love, ensuring each one is ready to become a part of a loving family.

Dogs

Our shelter is home to dogs of all shapes and sizes—from playful puppies to calm, mature companions. They’re waiting for active owners to give them walks, games, and affection.

Cats

Our cats are unique individuals! From lively kittens to cuddly lap cats and independent beauties, they’ll surely become your best companions.

Parrots

Colorful feathered friends are here to brighten your day with their cheerful chatter. We have both small budgies and larger parrots, each looking for a loving and interactive home.

Hamsters

Tiny, fluffy, and full of energy, hamsters are perfect for those who want a small and low-maintenance pet. A cozy habitat and a bit of attention are all they need to thrive.

Guinea Pigs

These social little creatures are great for both kids and adults. They love to play, interact, and enjoy your company.
